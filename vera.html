Our Story
BORN FROM STILLNESS
3+ Years of
Craft

Vera was founded in 2022 with a single belief: that a candle can transform a room — and a mood. Every scent we create begins as a feeling, not a formula.

Our candles are hand-poured in small batches using 100% natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and lead-free cotton wicks. We source responsibly and design with restraint.

We don't chase trends. We make objects that earn a permanent place in your space.
